How it works

From message → intent → tone → reply. One screen at a time.

  1. Share a message

    Open WhatsApp/SMS (or any app), select a message, tap Share, then choose Gazely.

  2. Pick a general intent

    Gazely suggests four high-level directions (e.g., answer, ask a question, decline, other). Use look & dwell to select.

  3. Narrow down details

    Choose from a specific set of options. Tap More… to see additional sets until you land on the right intent.

  4. Set the tone

    Select a tone (neutral, gentle, firm, urgent). Gazely can also combine eye-based emotion signals to help shape the final output.

  5. Pick a reply & refine

    Choose from multiple AI-crafted replies. Hit Refine to regenerate a new style, then copy/share it back.

Why 4 options?

Large targets are easier to select with gaze. Gazely keeps choices simple, then progressively reveals more detail only when you need it.

AI engine: Claude API (text generation). Platform: Android.

Designed for accessibility

Big tap targets, high contrast, and predictable steps — built to reduce cognitive load.

Accessibility-first

Gazely is built around a gaze-driven interface: fewer choices, bigger targets, and a consistent step-by-step flow.

  • Large 2×2 option grid for easy gaze selection
  • Progressive disclosure: only show what matters next
  • Clear typography and high-contrast surfaces
  • Optional touch fallback (when enabled) and simple backtracking

Designed to minimize data

Gazely runs when you explicitly share a message into the app. It does not continuously read notifications or monitor your chats.

  • You choose what text to share (manual transfer)
  • AI text generation via Claude API
  • We recommend publishing a clear privacy policy before launch
  • Security best practice: keep API keys off-device via a backend proxy

FAQ

Quick answers for launch pages, reviewers, and early users.

Who is Gazely for?
Anyone who finds typing difficult — especially people with motor impairments — and anyone who wants quick, clear replies.
Which apps does it work with?
Any app that supports Android’s Share menu (e.g., WhatsApp, SMS, email, messaging platforms). You share a message into Gazely.
Does Gazely read my messages automatically?
No — not in the current version. Message transfer is manual: you choose the message and share it into Gazely.
What powers the AI replies?
Text generation is powered by the Claude API. Your selected intent/tone guides the prompt to keep responses aligned.
